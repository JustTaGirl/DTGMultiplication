Step 1: Registration Image
-Enter Your First Name.

-Enter Your Last Name.

-Enter Your Date of Birth.

-Enter Your Email.

-Select Your Gender.

-Select Register to Complete Registration.



Step 2:Starting the Game Image
-Select The Start Button Start the Game.



Step 3:Playing The Game Image
-Multiply First Number By Second Number.

-Place Your Answer In the Box Provided.

-Click The Check Answer Button.

-If Your Answer is Correct The Answer Box Will Highlight Green.

-If Your Answer is Incorrect The Answer Box Will Highlight Red.



Step 4:View Your Score Image
-Select Next If you Wish To Move To Next Question.

-Select End If You Wish To End The Game And View Your Score.
